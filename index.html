Research areas

My research is in nonassociative algebra, especially polynomial identities and structural problems. I am interested in how algebraic identities influence the structure of different classes of algebras.

My current work is related to $n$-Lie algebras, Poisson and transposed Poisson algebras, Novikov algebras, Tortkara algebras, Zinbiel algebras, Jordan-type algebras, and operadic constructions.

I am also interested in questions about simplicity, nilpotency, solvability, cohomology, and central extensions.

About me

I was born and raised in Kazakhstan. My academic path through SDU University, Al-Farabi Kazakh National University, and Kazakh-British Technical University, and I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Southern University of Science and Technology in Shenzhen, China.

Besides research, I am interested in teaching mathematics, mentoring young students, and promoting algebra and fundamental science. I am open to collaborations in algebra and related fields.
